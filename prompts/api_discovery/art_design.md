# Art & Design - 公共 API 列表

- **[Icons8](https://img.icons8.com/)** - Icons (find "search icon" hyperlink in page) | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: Unknown

- **[Metropolitan Museum of Art](https://metmuseum.github.io/)** - Met Museum of Art | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: No
- **[PHP-Noise](https://php-noise.com/)** - Noise Background Image Generator | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: Yes
- **[Pixel Encounter](https://pixelencounter.com/api)** - SVG Icon Generator | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: No
