# Vehicle - 公共 API 列表

共 **6** 个 API

### 免认证 API（2 个）

- **[Brazilian Vehicles and Prices](https://deividfortuna.github.io/fipe/)** - Vehicles information from Fundação Instituto de Pesquisas Econômicas - Fipe | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: No
- **[NHTSA](https://vpic.nhtsa.dot.gov/api/)** - NHTSA Product Information Catalog and Vehicle Listing | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: Unknown

### 需认证 API（4 个）

- **[Helipaddy sites](https://helipaddy.com/api/)** - Helicopter and passenger drone landing site directory, Helipaddy data and much more | 认证: `apiKey` | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: Unknown
- **[Kelley Blue Book](http://developer.kbb.com/#!/data/1-Default)** - Vehicle info, pricing, configuration, plus much more | 认证: `apiKey` | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: No
- **[Mercedes-Benz](https://developer.mercedes-benz.com/apis)** - Telematics data, remotely access vehicle functions, car configurator, locate service dealers | 认证: `apiKey` | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: No
- **[Smartcar](https://smartcar.com/docs/)** - Lock and unlock vehicles and get data like odometer reading and location. Works on most new cars | 认证: `OAuth` | HTTPS: Yes | CORS: Yes
